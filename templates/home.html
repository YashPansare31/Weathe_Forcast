Parameter Definitions

  • Temperature: Air temperature in degrees Celsius.
  • Relative Humidity (RH): Percentage of atmospheric moisture.
  • Wind Speed (Ws): Wind speed in km/h.
  • Rainfall (Rain): Total rainfall in mm.
  • Fine Fuel Moisture Code (FFMC): Moisture content of surface fuels.
  • Duff Moisture Code (DMC): Moisture content of deeper organic layers.
  • Initial Spread Index (ISI): Fire spread potential.
  • Fire Weather Index Class (Classes): Classification of fire weather conditions.
  • Geographical Region: Location of the prediction area.
